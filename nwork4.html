NIKE AD CAMPAIGN | FITSYNC

The Fit Sync Challenge is a partnership between Nike and Spotify, aims to build fitness
habit by integrating 21 days fitness Challenge on the launch of Infinity shoes series.
Through exciting Rewards and dynamic imagery and compelling messaging, the design
should inspire participants to embrace a healthier lifestyle.

  • Objective: Build a fitness habit through a 21-day fitness challenge in partnership with Spotify Nike's Infinity shoes launch.
  • Concept: Active individuals seeking motivation and habit-building in their workouts.
  • Role: Created dynamic visuals and messaging that reflect the collaboration between Nike and Spotify
  • Key Message: Designed a campaign that promotes fitness and enjoyment through music, driving participation and engagement.
  • Outcome: Design a campaign
  • Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, InDesign
