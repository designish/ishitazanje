Poster Design

  • Objective: Create posters inspired by Rococo, Romanticism, and Post-Impressionism
    to connect historical art styles with modern product branding.
  • Concept: Blend the essence of each art movement into contemporary product designs,
    reflecting their unique visual and emotional appeal.
  • Role: Utilize color palettes, motifs, and design elements iconic to the chosen art movements.
  • Key Message: Emphasize how art movements can enhance branding by evoking emotional connections and aesthetic appeal.
  • Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
